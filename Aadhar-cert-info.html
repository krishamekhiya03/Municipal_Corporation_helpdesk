Aadhar Enrollment/Correction
➩ How to Apply for Aadhar Enrollment/Correction Offline in GMC ?

  • Step 1: Locate An Enrollment Centre.
  • Step 2: Fill The Form.
  • Step 3: Submit The Required Documents.
  • Step 4: Provide Demographic & Biographic Details.
  • Step 5: Get The Acknowledgement Slip.
Required Document

  • Proof of Identity (POI) Documents - Comprising Name and Photograph
    • Passport
    • PAN Card
    • Ration/ PDS Photo Card
    • Voter ID
    • Driving License
  • Proof of Date of Birth (DOB) Documents for Aadhar Card of Family if Required
    • Birth Certificate
    • SSLC Book/ Certificate
    • Passport
    • Certificate of Date of Birth issued by Group A Gazetted Officer on UIDAI standard certificate format for enrolment/ update
    • PAN Card
  • Proof of Address (POA) Documents for Aadhar Card - Comprising Name and Address
    • Passport
    • Bank Statement/ Passbook
    • Post Office Account Statement/ Passbook
    • Ration Card
    • Driving License
